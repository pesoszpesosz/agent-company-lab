## Review Checklist Before Any Listing

Browser read-only marketplace terms review:

- Are Markdown template bundles allowed?
- What file formats and file-size limits apply?
- What refund, dispute, and support obligations apply?
- What fees, payout thresholds, country restrictions, and tax collection rules apply?
- Are AI-agent, prompt, or workflow-template claims restricted?

Legal/KYC/tax/payment review:

- Which seller identity should be used, if any?
- What tax/KYC/payment setup would be required?
- What refund/license wording should be approved?
- What payout method is acceptable?
- Are there local jurisdiction restrictions or reporting obligations?

IP/license/claim review:

- Is all text original to the bundle?
- Are platform names used only descriptively?
- Does the buyer license avoid overpromising compliance or results?
- Are screenshots free of private, signed-in, or third-party marketplace content?

Public-action listing approval:

- Which exact platform route is approved?
- What exact title, description, price, refund wording, and screenshots are approved?
- Who performs the upload and seller-account action?
- What post-listing support boundary is approved?
