## Gate Map

- Channel creation: account registration and terms gate.
- Uploads: public-action approval, copyright review, exact video asset packet.
- Thumbnails: copyright/brand review and public-action approval before use.
- Voice/music/footage: licensing and attribution review.
- Comments/community: public-action and reputation review.
- Monetization/sponsors/affiliate links: legal, tax, payout, account, and public-action gates.

## First Local Proof Definition

The first proof is complete when there is a `youtube_no_post_content_batch_v1` containing:

- 10 scripts or structured script briefs.
- 30 title variants.
- 10 thumbnail briefs.
- B-roll/source plan with licensing notes.
- Voiceover/editor options with cost and gate notes.
- One production manifest.
- One reputation/copyright review checklist.
