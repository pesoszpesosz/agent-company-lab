## Checklist

Before any public-action packet is prepared:

- Every factual claim has a source or is framed as internal build commentary.
- No customer-provided video is copied, mirrored, or paraphrased too closely.
- No third-party thumbnail, logo, music, clip, chart, screenshot, or voice is used without license or review.
- No implied revenue, customer result, sponsorship, certification, or endorsement is claimed without evidence.
- No financial, legal, security, or tax advice is presented as professional advice.
- No exploit, vulnerability, or target-specific security detail is publicized.
- No account, upload, comment, reply, monetization, or affiliate action is included.
- The video has a clear audience, promise, and safe CTA.
- The script can stand alone without private company context.
- The public-action route is explicit if the piece is ever promoted for upload.
