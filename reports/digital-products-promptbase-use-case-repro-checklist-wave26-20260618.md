## Local Use Case

Agent Skill Starter Kit v0 helps indie AI builders and small technical teams turn a repeatable local AI-agent workflow into a safer reusable skill packet with a `SKILL.md` template, stop-gate checklist, service-request checklist, artifact contract, acceptance-review template, and fictional local example.

It is useful when the buyer needs structure for local agent work and wants explicit boundaries around accounts, payments, browser actions, credentials, public posts, APIs, legal/tax/KYC, and external side effects.
